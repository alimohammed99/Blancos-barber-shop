Crew Cut

Expert Barber

A crew cut is a type of haircut in which the upright hair
on the top of the head is cut relatively short, graduated
in length from the longest hair that forms a short pomp
at the front hairline to the shortest at the back of the crown.

The crew cut also known as the ivy league haircut is similar
to the buzz cut but slightly longer in length.

Regular Hair Cut

Expert Barber

A regular haircut is men's and boys'hairstyle that has
hair long enough to comb on top, a defined or deconstructed
side part, and a short, semi-short, medium, long or extra long
back and sides.

This style is also known by other names including taper cut,
regular taper cut, side-part and standard haircut.

Hair Color

Expert Barber

Hair color is the pigmentation of hair follicles due to
two types of melanin: eumelanin and pheomelanin.
Generally, if more eumelanin is present, the color
of the hair is darker and if less eumelanin is present,
the hair is lighter.
